Alpaca

Alpacas are similar to llamas, and are often confused with them. The two animals are closely related, and can successfully cross-breed.

  • Scientific Name: Vicugna pacos
  • Average Lengh: 81–99 centimetres in height at the shoulders
  • Average Lifespan: 15–20 years
  • Habitat: South America.

Alpacas can be found all over South America. Since they are currently domesticated, they are no longer living anywhere in the wild.
